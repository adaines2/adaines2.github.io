Moms trust other Moms

Motherhood is a life-changing experience, that is filled with love, joy and triumphs. It also comes with anxieties, uncertainties and setbacks, which are often conquered communally with the advice and support of other moms. For those who have children with developmental challenges, the motherhood journey may become more fearful, unknown, and lonely, as fewer mothers are going through similar obstacles. Seeking guidance from the community of other moms becomes more difficult, making it harder to do what’s best for their child.
